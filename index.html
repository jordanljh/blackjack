Rules:

1. Bigger hand value, not exceeding 21, will win.

2. Face cards are worth 10, while Ace is worth 1 or 11.

3. Hit to draw more cards or stand to stop.

4. Dealer will draw when hand is 16 or less

5. Blackjack payout 1.5x of bet, all other wins or losses are 1x.

6. Game will start with 100 credits and ends with 0 credits.
